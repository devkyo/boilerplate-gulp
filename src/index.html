About us

MUFF TRADING is a commodity trading boutique founded in 2018 with Headquarters in Baar, Switzerland. We are specialists in physical trading of precious metals & non-ferrous raw materials out of South America. Over the years our team has established a worldwide network among mining companies, traders and consumers. Through our well-established network we source and market our products.
